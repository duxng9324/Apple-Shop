TAI LIEU PHAN TICH NGHIEP VU VA KY THUAT - DU AN APPLESHOP

1) TONG QUAN
- Du an gom 3 thanh phan: React Frontend, Java Spring Boot Backend, Python FastAPI Chatbot.
- Muc tieu: ban hang online + van hanh kho + nghiep vu ke toan co ban + chatbot AI.

2) NGHIEP VU CHINH
- Quan ly nguoi dung va phan quyen.
- Quan ly danh muc san pham: category, color, memory, product.
- Gio hang, dat hang, cap nhat thanh toan.
- Kho: lap phieu nhap, phieu xuat, dieu chinh ton kho, tru ton FIFO/LIFO.
- Ke toan: report, journal, AR/AP aging, reconciliation, phieu thu, phieu chi, dashboard.
- Chatbot AI: tu van san pham va huong dan mua hang.

3) USE CASE CHINH
- Customer: dang nhap, xem san pham, them gio, dat hang, thanh toan, chat AI.
- Product Manager: CRUD danh muc va san pham.
- Warehouse Manager: nhap xuat kho, dieu chinh ton, xac nhan don.
- Accountant: xem bao cao, cong no, nhat ky, xuat excel.
- Admin: quan tri tong the va phan quyen.

4) THIET KE DATABASE (TOM TAT)
- Nhom ban hang: user, product, category, color, memory, product_memory, product_image, cart, orders, order_item.
- Nhom kho: warehouse, inventory, stock_receipt, stock_receipt_item, stock_issue, stock_issue_item, inventory_movement_ledger.
- Nhom ke toan: chart_of_account, journal_entry, journal_line, receivable, payable, payment_transaction, expense_voucher.
- Quan he chinh:
  + user 1-n orders
  + orders 1-n order_item
  + warehouse 1-n inventory
  + stock_receipt 1-n stock_receipt_item
  + stock_issue 1-n stock_issue_item
  + journal_entry 1-n journal_line

5) KY THUAT CHINH
- Frontend: React, Ant Design, Recharts, Axios, XLSX.
- Backend: Spring Boot, Spring Security, JPA, MySQL, JWT.
- Chatbot: FastAPI, Redis, Ollama.
- Bao mat: role-based access control + JWT.

6) TEST CASE GOI Y
- Chuc nang:
  + Dang nhap dung sai.
  + Tao don hang.
  + Confirm don theo FIFO/LIFO.
  + Kiem tra ton kho khong am.
  + Bao cao ke toan theo ngay.
  + Xuat excel phieu thu phieu chi.
- Bao mat:
  + Khong token -> 401.
  + Sai quyen -> 403.
- Dong bo:
  + Hai nguoi cung cap nhat ton kho -> conflict co kiem soat.

7) CAC BIEU DO KY THUAT NEN DUA VAO BAO CAO
- Use case diagram tong quan actor va nghiep vu.
- Architecture diagram frontend backend chatbot redis mysql.
- Sequence diagram dat hang va tru ton FIFO/LIFO.

8) TAI LIEU KY THUAT CAN KEM
- Mo ta API theo nhom module.
- Mo ta role permission.
- Mo ta quy tac tinh doanh thu gia von loi nhuan.
- Mo ta quy trinh xuat bao cao excel.
- Mo ta quy trinh chatbot va session memory Redis.

10) PHAN TICH CHI TIET TUNG USE CASE

UC01 Dang ky dang nhap
- Muc tieu: tao tai khoan va dang nhap an toan bang JWT.
- Tien dieu kien: thong tin dang ky hop le.
- Luong chinh: nhap thong tin -> validate -> xac thuc -> cap token.
- Ngoai le: trung tai khoan, sai mat khau, token het han.

UC02 Xem tim kiem san pham
- Muc tieu: tim san pham theo bo loc.
- Tien dieu kien: co du lieu product va danh muc.
- Luong chinh: mo danh sach -> loc/tim -> xem ket qua phan trang.
- Ngoai le: khong co ket qua, loi API.

UC03 Quan ly gio hang
- Muc tieu: them sua xoa san pham trong gio.
- Tien dieu kien: da dang nhap.
- Luong chinh: chon bien the -> them gio -> cap nhat so luong -> tinh tong tien.
- Ngoai le: vuot ton kho, san pham ngung ban.

UC04 Dat hang
- Muc tieu: tao don tu gio hang.
- Tien dieu kien: gio hang khong rong.
- Luong chinh: nhap thong tin nhan -> tao order -> tao order_item -> tra ket qua.
- Ngoai le: thieu thong tin giao nhan, gio hang khong hop le.

UC05 Xac nhan thanh toan
- Muc tieu: cap nhat payment_status va giao dich thu tien.
- Tien dieu kien: don da tao.
- Luong chinh: nhan ket qua thanh toan -> xac thuc -> ghi payment_transaction.
- Ngoai le: ma giao dich trung, amount sai.

UC06 Quan ly danh muc san pham
- Muc tieu: CRUD category color memory product.
- Tien dieu kien: co quyen PRODUCT hoac ADMIN.
- Luong chinh: tao/sua/xoa danh muc -> tao/sua product.
- Ngoai le: trung ma, xoa du lieu dang duoc tham chieu.

UC07 Lap phieu nhap kho
- Muc tieu: tang ton kho theo phieu nhap.
- Tien dieu kien: co kho va item hop le.
- Luong chinh: tao phieu -> validate item -> luu phieu -> cap nhat inventory.
- Ngoai le: so luong am, kho khong ton tai.

UC08 Lap phieu xuat kho
- Muc tieu: giam ton kho theo phieu xuat.
- Tien dieu kien: ton kho du.
- Luong chinh: tao phieu -> kiem tra ton -> luu phieu -> tru ton.
- Ngoai le: ton kho khong du.

UC09 Dieu chinh ton kho
- Muc tieu: sua ton khi kiem ke chenh lech.
- Tien dieu kien: co bien ban dieu chinh.
- Luong chinh: chon ban ghi ton -> nhap muc dieu chinh -> cap nhat ton -> luu log.
- Ngoai le: ton am sau dieu chinh, thieu ly do.

UC10 Xac nhan don va tru ton FIFO/LIFO
- Muc tieu: chot don va tru ton theo chien luoc.
- Tien dieu kien: don cho xac nhan va ton du.
- Luong chinh: chon FIFO/LIFO -> phan bo lot -> tao phieu xuat -> cap nhat order.
- Ngoai le: khong du lot, xung dot cap nhat dong thoi.

UC11 Xem bao cao ke toan
- Muc tieu: theo doi revenue cogs expense profit.
- Tien dieu kien: co du lieu phat sinh.
- Luong chinh: chon khoang ngay -> tong hop KPI -> hien thi bieu do.
- Ngoai le: ngay khong hop le, khong co du lieu.

UC12 Xem cong no AR/AP
- Muc tieu: theo doi phai thu phai tra theo tuoi no.
- Tien dieu kien: co receivable payable dang mo.
- Luong chinh: chon asOfDate -> tinh overdue -> gom bucket -> hien thi danh sach.
- Ngoai le: du lieu den han thieu hoac khong hop le.

UC13 Xem nhat ky ke toan
- Muc tieu: doi soat but toan no co.
- Tien dieu kien: co journal entry line.
- Luong chinh: loc theo ngay/chung tu/tai khoan -> hien thi no co.
- Ngoai le: but toan mat can doi.

UC14 Xuat excel phieu thu chi va bao cao
- Muc tieu: xuat file excel de doi soat va nop bao cao.
- Tien dieu kien: co du lieu dashboard va voucher.
- Luong chinh: chon loai xuat -> lay du lieu -> tao workbook -> tai file.
- Ngoai le: loi API, khong co du lieu.

UC15 Tu van AI
- Muc tieu: hoi dap nhanh ve san pham mua hang.
- Tien dieu kien: chatbot, redis, llm hoat dong.
- Luong chinh: gui cau hoi -> phan tich intent -> lay memory -> goi llm -> tra loi.
- Ngoai le: mat ket noi redis/llm, cau hoi ngoai pham vi.
